About Us

Engineering Excellence. Racing Passion.

Our Mission

Burnout Motors is a university club dedicated to the art and science of automotive engineering. We combine academic excellence with hands-on experience, building and customizing BMW cars equipped with powerful V8 engines for competitive GT3 racing in Norway.

What We Do

Our team of passionate students works tirelessly to design, build, and optimize high-performance racing machines. From engine tuning to aerodynamics, every aspect of our vehicles is carefully engineered to achieve peak performance on the track.

Our Journey

Starting as a small group of automotive enthusiasts, Burnout Motors has grown into a recognized force in Norwegian GT3 racing. We compete in various racing series, showcasing not just our engineering skills but also our dedication to excellence and teamwork.
